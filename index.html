About Us

Siraat Academy offers online Quran Recitation and Tajweed classes for students of all ages and backgrounds — beginners to advanced. Our mission is to make authentic Quranic education accessible to everyone, anywhere in the world, taught by qualified Alim teachers, Maulanas, and Hazrats.

Our Courses

Tajweed Course

Learn the correct pronunciation and rules of recitation, step by step, with personal guidance from experienced teachers.

Quran Recitation

Improve your Quran reading fluency with one-on-one or group online sessions, suitable for kids and adults alike.

Flexible Scheduling

Class timings are arranged according to each student's convenience — we work around your schedule.

Our Teachers

Our classes are taught by qualified Alim teachers, Maulanas, and Hazrats with strong knowledge of Quranic Tajweed and recitation, ensuring authentic and reliable learning for every student.

Fees

We believe everyone should have the opportunity to learn the Quran. Fees are flexible and based on what each candidate can afford — contact us to discuss what works for you. No one is turned away due to financial constraints.
