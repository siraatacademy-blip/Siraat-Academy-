Welcome

A Path of Light, Open to Everyone

"Siraat" means "the straight path" — and our mission is to make that path of Qur'anic learning accessible to every household, regardless of background or budget. Whether you are a complete beginner or wish to refine your recitation, our teachers meet you exactly where you are.

What We Teach

Our Classes

Small, focused, one-on-one or group sessions — conducted entirely online, scheduled around your life.

Tajweed Course

Master the rules of correct pronunciation — letter articulation, elongation, stopping rules — so every word of the Qur'an is recited as it was revealed.

Beginners & Advanced

Qur'an Recitation

Build fluency and confidence reading the Qur'an, with patient correction and steady progress — suitable for children, adults and seniors alike.

All Ages

Flexible Timing

Class schedules are arranged around each student's availability — early morning, evening, or weekend, anywhere across time zones.

Built Around You

Fees by Sincerity, Not by Price Tag

We believe no one should be turned away from learning the Qur'an because of money. Fees are arranged according to what each student or family can comfortably afford — speak with us openly, and we'll find a way.
