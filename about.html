Our Story

About Siraat Academy

We are dedicated to spreading the beauty of Quranic recitation to every corner of the world — one student at a time.

Our Mission

Teaching the Quran
the Right Way

Siraat Academy was founded with a single, heartfelt purpose: to make proper Quranic education accessible to every Muslim around the world, regardless of age, location, or background.

The word "Siraat" means path — and we believe that learning the Quran with Tajweed is the most blessed path a person can walk. We guide students step by step on that journey with patience, expertise, and dedication.

📚

Knowledge-First Approach

We build deep understanding of Tajweed rules, not just rote recitation.

🤲

Authentic Islamic Teaching

Our methods follow the traditional chain of Quranic transmission (Isnad).

💡

Modern Learning Tools

Interactive online sessions with screen sharing, recordings, and progress tracking.

❤️

Nurturing Environment

Every student is treated with respect and encouragement, no matter their level.
