Our Story & Our Teachers

About Siraat Academy

A small academy with a simple aim — make authentic Qur'anic education available to anyone, anywhere, regardless of means.

Our Mission

Why "Siraat"?

Siraat means "the path" — the straight path referred to in Surah Al-Fatiha. We chose this name because we see Tajweed and Qur'an learning not as an end in itself, but as a path: one that should be open, well-lit, and walkable by every person, at their own pace, regardless of age, location, or financial circumstance.

Our Teachers

Guided by Qualified Scholars

Every class at Siraat Academy is conducted by qualified Alim teachers, Maulanas and Hazrats — individuals who have devoted years to the study of the Qur'an, Tajweed, and Islamic sciences.

Deep Knowledge

Our teachers hold formal qualifications (Alim/Hifz/Tajweed ijaza-level training) from recognized institutions, ensuring what you learn is correct and authentic.

Patient Teaching

Many of our students are complete beginners — children, working adults, new Muslims. Our teachers are experienced in starting from zero and building confidence slowly.

Personal Attention

With small class sizes and one-on-one options, every student receives direct correction and feedback — not just a lecture.

Our Approach

What Makes Us Different

Open to Everyone

We welcome students of all ages and all levels — total beginners, those returning to learning after years, and those polishing advanced recitation. No one is too old, too young, or "too behind" to start.

Fees Without Barriers

We do not want cost to be the reason someone cannot learn the Qur'an. Fees are discussed individually and set according to what each family can genuinely afford — with full sincerity on both sides.

Join Us on This Path

Wherever you are in your journey with the Qur'an — just starting the alphabet, or refining years of recitation — there is a place for you here.
