What We Offer

Our Courses

Structured, patient, one-step-at-a-time learning — guided entirely by qualified teachers, online.

Tajweed Course

A complete, step-by-step study of the rules governing correct Qur'anic recitation — from the basics of Arabic letter sounds to advanced rules of elongation, nasalization, and pause.

  • Correct articulation points (makharij) of each letter
  • Rules of Noon & Meem (Ikhfa, Idgham, Iqlab, Izhar)
  • Rules of stretching (Madd) and stopping (Waqf)
  • Practical application through guided Qur'an reading
  • One-on-one correction and feedback every session
Suitable for Beginners & Advanced Students

Qur'an Recitation

Focused practice in reading the Qur'an itself — building fluency, confidence, and a beautiful, melodious recitation (tarteel), at a pace that suits you.

  • Letter recognition and reading practice (for new learners)
  • Word-by-word and verse-by-verse recitation practice
  • Memorization (Hifz) support on request
  • Gentle correction with repetition until mastery
  • Progress tracked session by session
