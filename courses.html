Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior knowledge to join?

Not at all! Our beginner courses start from absolute zero. We welcome complete beginners of all ages.

What platform do you use for classes?

We primarily use Zoom and Google Meet. You just need a smartphone or laptop with a stable internet connection.

Is the free trial class really free?

Yes, 100% free with no strings attached. You can experience our teaching quality before committing to any plan.

Can I reschedule a class if something comes up?

Yes, we understand life gets busy. You can reschedule by informing us at least 12 hours in advance via WhatsApp.

Do you teach students from non-Arabic speaking countries?

Absolutely! We teach students from all over the world. Classes are conducted in Urdu and English.
